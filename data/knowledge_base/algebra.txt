# Algebra — Key Formulas and Concepts for JEE

## Quadratic Equations
A quadratic equation has the form ax² + bx + c = 0 where a ≠ 0.

### Quadratic Formula
x = (-b ± √(b² - 4ac)) / (2a)

### Discriminant
D = b² - 4ac
- D > 0: Two distinct real roots
- D = 0: Two equal real roots (one repeated root)
- D < 0: No real roots (two complex conjugate roots)

### Vieta's Formulas
For ax² + bx + c = 0 with roots α, β:
- Sum of roots: α + β = -b/a
- Product of roots: αβ = c/a

## Arithmetic Progression (AP)
- nth term: aₙ = a + (n-1)d
- Sum of n terms: Sₙ = n/2 × (2a + (n-1)d) = n/2 × (first + last)

## Geometric Progression (GP)
- nth term: aₙ = a × rⁿ⁻¹
- Sum of n terms: Sₙ = a(rⁿ - 1)/(r - 1) for r ≠ 1
- Sum to infinity (|r| < 1): S∞ = a/(1-r)

## Binomial Theorem
(a + b)ⁿ = Σ C(n,k) × aⁿ⁻ᵏ × bᵏ for k = 0 to n
where C(n,k) = n! / (k!(n-k)!)

General term: T(r+1) = C(n,r) × aⁿ⁻ʳ × bʳ

## Logarithms
- log_a(xy) = log_a(x) + log_a(y)
- log_a(x/y) = log_a(x) - log_a(y)
- log_a(xⁿ) = n × log_a(x)
- Change of base: log_a(x) = log_b(x) / log_b(a)

## Inequalities
- AM ≥ GM ≥ HM for positive reals
- AM = (a+b)/2, GM = √(ab), HM = 2ab/(a+b)
- Cauchy-Schwarz: (Σaᵢbᵢ)² ≤ (Σaᵢ²)(Σbᵢ²)

## Polynomials
- Factor theorem: (x-a) is a factor of P(x) iff P(a) = 0
- Remainder theorem: P(x) divided by (x-a) gives remainder P(a)
- Fundamental theorem of algebra: degree n polynomial has exactly n roots (counting multiplicity, in ℂ)
